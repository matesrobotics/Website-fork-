Welcome to the MATES Robotics Website!

What is MATES Robotics?

MATES stands for the Marine Academy of Technology and Environmental Science. It is a STEM-based vocational school in Ocean County.

We participate in the First Tech Challenge where we compete with other schools to build the best robot for the yearly challenge. Our rookie year was the 2016-2017 season and we've only improved since then.

This website is currently a work in progress by our members as we focus on constructing the robot for the upcoming season.
